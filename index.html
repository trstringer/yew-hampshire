What do we stand for?

We are a group of surfers 🏄 that ride waves 🌊 in New Hampshire and northern New England. We surf in July ☀️ and January ❄️ , dawn patrol 🌅 and last light. We surf when it's hot 🔥 and when it's cold 🥶. When the waves are small, and when the waves are big (if it's safe, and if that's your thing). We are all different levels of surfers, from learning to shredding. The best surfer is the one having the most fun.

Surfing is a large part of our lives, but when there are other committments that is perfectly fine as well. When we can get together for a surf session, we do. And we like to have fun on the land too (when it's safe with COVID) with anything ranging from a backyard BBQ 🍔 to other fun sports and activities 🏂 ⛷ 🚵.

How can you join?

Send an email 📧 to membership@yewhampshire.com to request membership including the following information:

📛 Your name
🌎 What town/city you live in
❓ Why you want to join

This is no-cost (zero dollar 💰), low committment group. Your contributions should be your good vibes and happy smile. Note, this group is meant for surfers that are 21+ years old.
