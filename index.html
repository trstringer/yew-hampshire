What do we stand for?

We are a group of surfers 🏄 that ride waves 🌊 in New Hampshire and northern New England. We surf in July ☀️ and January ❄️ , dawn patrol 🌅 and last light. We surf when it's hot 🔥 and when it's cold 🥶. When the waves are small, and when the waves are big (if it's safe). We are all different levels of surfers, from learning to shredding. The best surfer is the one having the most fun.

Surfing is not everything, though. We make sure we have our priorities straight, so if there is a family or work thing going on we might not paddle out. But when we are available for some fun, we'll be catching waves or having some fun on land together.
